About Bunks

What is Bunks?

Bunks connects people with places to stay and things to do around Lagos. The community is powered by our affordable apartments, community hosts, and hostel owners who provide their guests with affordable stays and excellent services.

What is hosting?

If you have an extra room, entire home, or expertise, you can earn money by sharing it with anyone around Nigeria. You can host your home, activity, or do both. When you host is up to you.

Frequently asked questions

Who can be a Bunks host?
It’s easy to become a Bunks host in most areas, and it’s always free to create a listing. Entire apartments and homes, private rooms, treehouses, and castles are just a few of the properties hosts have shared on Bunks.

For more details on what’s expected of hosts, check out Bunks' community standards, which revolve around safety, security, and reliability, and hospitality standards, which help hosts earn great guest reviews.
What is required of guests before booking?
We ask everyone who uses Bunks for a few pieces of information before they book with us. Guests need to have this info completely filled out before they can make a reservation request. This info helps make sure you know who to expect, and how to contact the guest.

Bunks' requirements for guests include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Confirmed phone number
  • Introductory message
  • Agreement to your House Rules
  • Payment information
How much does it cost to list my space?
Signing up for Bunks and listing your home is completely free.

Once you receive a reservation, we charge an Bunks service fee for hosts, generally 3%, to help cover the cost of running of the business.
How should I choose my listing’s price?
The price you charge for your listing is completely up to you. To help you decide, you can search for comparable listings in your city or neighborhood to get an idea of market prices.
